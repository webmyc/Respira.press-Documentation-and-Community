---
title: respira sites
description: Reference for respira sites list, info, health, connect, and disconnect.
---

Manage the WordPress sites in your workspace.

```bash
respira sites list [--builder=elementor] [--status=connected]
respira sites info <site>
respira sites health <site>
respira sites connect <url>
respira sites disconnect <site> --confirm
```

Sites are referenced by URL or ID. The CLI accepts either form on every command.

## Filtering

`list` accepts filters:
- `--builder=<name>`: one of `elementor`, `divi`, `bricks`, `wpbakery`, `beaver`, `oxygen`, `breakdance`, `brizy`, `thrive`, `flatsome`, `gutenberg`
- `--status=<state>`: `connected`, `disconnected`, `error`
- `--search=<term>`: text search across site names and URLs
