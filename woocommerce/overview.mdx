## Overview

Respira Commerce extends Respira's safety-first workflow to your WooCommerce store. 36 tools that connect commerce data to page builder visuals with snapshots on every write.

**Pricing:**
- **Studio and Founder plans:** included free, no separate purchase
- **Maker and Builder plans:** €95/year as a separate add-on

No free trial on the WooCommerce add-on. The core Respira plugin has a 7-day trial, but the WooCommerce add-on is paid from day one.

- Respira Core installed and connected (Maker, Builder, Studio, or Founder plan)

- PHP 7.4 or newer

## What the add-on includes

### Original tools (21)

The foundation: products, orders, categories, tags, inventory, and reports.

- **Products:** create, read, update, duplicate simple and variable products
- **Categories and tags:** full CRUD for product taxonomy
- **Orders:** list, filter, update status (triggers WooCommerce emails)
- **Inventory:** stock levels, stock status, low-stock detection
- **Reports:** sales by period, top products, revenue tracking

### New in v6.0: Commerce tools (15)

The layer above CRUD. Tools that bridge commerce data and page builder visuals.

**Storefront design intelligence (6 tools):**
- `analyze_shop_page` — read your shop page in the active builder format, return hierarchy assessment and actionable issues
- `analyze_product_page` — image, description, price, and category quality scoring
- `update_product_card_layout` — modify product grid columns, rows, ordering via builder abstraction
- `add_low_stock_badge` — identify products needing low-stock badges with configurable threshold
- `add_sale_badge` — calculate discount percentages for sale badges
- `update_checkout_layout` — modify checkout page via builder abstraction

Storefront intelligence is supported for Elementor, Divi, Flatsome, Gutenberg, and Bricks in v6.0. Other builders return a clear unsupported message.

**Catalog operations (4 tools):**
- `list_products_advanced` — filter by category, tag, stock status, sale, featured, price range
- `bulk_update_products` — batch product updates with 100-product cap and snapshots
- `analyze_catalog_health` — audit for missing images, short descriptions, no category, zero-stock
- `find_product_by_natural_language` — "the blue cotton t-shirt on sale" returns scored matches

**Pricing (3 tools):**
- `bulk_update_prices` — percentage, absolute, or set-value changes with dry_run and snapshots
- `schedule_sale` — set sale price plus start/end dates with validation
- `revert_pricing` — roll back to a Respira snapshot

**Inventory (2 tools):**
- `bulk_update_stock` — set, increase, or decrease stock with 100-product cap and snapshots
- `find_low_stock` — products below configurable threshold

## Safety

Every WooCommerce write operation creates a before/after snapshot. Every bulk operation has dry_run mode. 100-product hard cap per call prevents runaway AI mistakes. PII is redacted by default on order data.

- **Snapshots:** every product write (create, update, stock change) captures before and after states
- **Dry-run:** bulk_update_prices, bulk_update_products, bulk_update_stock, schedule_sale all support `dry_run: true`
- **Hard cap:** maximum 100 products per bulk call
- **Audit trail:** all commerce writes logged to the Respira audit log
- **PII redaction:** customer email, phone, and address redacted by default on order endpoints

<Callout kind="tip">

The financial core of your store stays under WooCommerce and your payment gateway. Respira stages and snapshots changes. You confirm important updates yourself.

</Callout>
